Welcome to Arya Samaj Mandir, Vasant Kunj

The Arya Samaj is a religious and socio-cultural reformatory organisation which was founded by Maharishi Swami Dayanand Saraswati in 1875 at Mumbai, India. Today, Arya Samaj has branches and establishments in many countries of the world with presence in major cities of India, USA, Canada, UK amongst others.

Donation – Options

Generous Donations are solicited for construction of the Bhawan. The Samaj shall acknowledge the donations as indicated below:

Rs. 5 lacs and more:  

Names will be very prominently displayed on Ground Floor. Donors shall be eligible to be Yajman at three havans every year and also attend Donors Retreat.

Rs. 3 lacs to Rs 5 lacs:

Names will be prominently displayed on Ground Floor or First floor. Donors shall be eligible to be Yajman at two havans every year and also attend Donors Retreat.

Rs. 1 lac to Rs 3 lacs: 

Names will be displayed on Ground or First Floor. Donors shall be eligible to be Yajman at one havan every year and also attend Donors Retreat.

Less than Rs. 1 lac:  

A special register will be maintained of all donors to recognize their contribution and will be available in the Library of the Arya Samaj.
